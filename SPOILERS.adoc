== Charms
=== Moss-Mask
A charm found at Queens-Gardens Toll. +
Makes Players survive one hit, that would otherwise kill them.

=== Painters Spirit
A charm found at the Mask Maker's room. +
It increases the Players Nail Arts (except Cyclone-Slash).

| 21 | 32 | 53 | 74
